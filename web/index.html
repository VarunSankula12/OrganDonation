What We Do

Kidney Transplantations

A healthy kidney from a donor is implanted into a person with kidney failure. This life-saving procedure can significantly improve the recipient's quality of life and overall health.

Read More
Lungs Transplantations

Lung transplantation is a surgical procedure in which one or both diseased or failing lungs are replaced with healthy lungs from a deceased donor, typically done to treat advanced lung diseases and improve respiratory function.

Read More
Liver Transplantations

A liver transplant involves the surgical replacement of a damaged or diseased liver with a healthy liver from a deceased or living donor to treat end-stage liver disease and improve the recipient's health.

Read More
Heart Transplantations

A heart transplant is a surgical procedure in which a diseased or failing heart is replaced with a healthy donor heart to improve the recipient's cardiac function and overall quality of life.

Read More
Eyes Transplantations

Corneal transplantation, also known as corneal grafting or keratoplasty, involves replacing a damaged or diseased cornea with a healthy donor cornea.
